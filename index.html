About Us

Since 1991, Regal Heights Marble has been the premier choice for discerning clients seeking exceptional tile, natural stone, and marble installation.

Founded by David Reis, our company has built an unparalleled reputation for precision craftsmanship, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. With over three decades of specialized experience, we have successfully completed countless residential and commercial projects throughout the Greater Toronto Area and across Ontario.

Our team of master craftsmen combines traditional artisan techniques with modern installation methods to deliver results that exceed industry standards. We take pride in transforming spaces with the timeless elegance of tile, natural stone, and marble ensuring every project reflects our dedication to excellence.
